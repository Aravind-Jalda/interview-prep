# Troubleshooting Interview Questions & Answers (DevOps - 2 to 5 Years)

---

## 1. What is troubleshooting in DevOps?
Troubleshooting is the process of identifying, analyzing, and fixing issues in systems or applications.

---

## 2. What is your general troubleshooting approach?

Step-by-step:
1. Understand the problem  
2. Check logs  
3. Verify system status  
4. Check dependencies  
5. Fix and validate  

---

## 3. Why logs are important?
Logs help identify errors and understand system behavior.

---

## 4. What tools do you use for troubleshooting?

- Linux commands (top, ps, netstat)  
- Docker logs  
- kubectl logs  
- Monitoring tools (Prometheus, Grafana)  

---

## 5. What is the first step when issue occurs?
Understand the problem clearly and reproduce if possible.

---

## 6. How do you identify root cause?
- Analyze logs  
- Check recent changes  
- Verify dependencies  

---

## 7. What is root cause analysis (RCA)?
Finding the actual reason behind a problem, not just fixing symptoms.

---

## 8. How do you handle production issues?
- Stay calm  
- Identify impact  
- Fix quickly  
- Document RCA  

---

## 9. What is monitoring role in troubleshooting?
Monitoring helps detect issues early using alerts and metrics.

---

## 10. What is difference between logs and metrics?
- Logs → detailed events  
- Metrics → numerical data  

---

## 11. What is alerting?
Alerting notifies when system crosses threshold.

---

## 12. What is rollback?
Reverting system to previous stable state.

---

## 13. How do you debug performance issue?
- Check CPU/memory  
- Check network  
- Analyze logs  

---

## 14. How do you debug deployment failure?
- Check pipeline logs  
- Check configuration  
- Verify environment  

---

## 15. How do you handle unknown issues?
- Break into smaller parts  
- Test each component  
- Narrow down problem  

---

## 16. What is dependency check?
Verify all dependent services are working.

---

## 17. What is health check?
Check if application is running properly.

---

## 18. How do you prevent issues?
- Monitoring  
- Alerts  
- Automation  
- Testing  

---

## 19. What is incident management?
Handling and resolving production issues.

---

## 20. What is post-incident review?
Analyze issue after resolution to prevent future problems.

---
